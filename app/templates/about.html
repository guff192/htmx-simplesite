About

Imagine a simpler time, when listening to music involved little plastic casings called cassettes, or maybe a little later, when music was contained in miniature frisbees with a hole in the middle (aka, CDs).

Back then, our music libraries were contained in a physical space, whether a cassette tape rack, or those old CD binders with sleeves for the awesome CD art (and occasional lyrics). And let's not forget about those awesome mixes we used to make.

Now a days, all we have are songs somewhere on the "cloud" and our "mixes" have become "playlists". Blah.

But at the very least, we can build little apps to emulate the feeling of having our music all in one place. Sure, you can do this within your favorite streaming app as well, but then again, why not make your own using a little Python, htmx, and TailwindCSS?
